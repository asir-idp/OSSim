Example: Pagination (Page Size 2 units)

Processes are divided into fixed-sized pages (in Example 2 units), memory is also divided into frames of equal size. Process pages are allocated into available memory frames.


Process Size Duration
Time
P1 5 u. (3 pages) 4
P2 4 u. (2 pages) ∞
P3 11 u. (6 pages) 2
P4 5 u. (3 pages) ∞
P5 7 u. (4 pages) ∞
P6 3 u. (2 pages) ∞

Processes are allocated in the table order.

Comments:

  • The last page of a processes is not always fully occupied, this creates internal fragmentation
  • On pagination it makes no sense to talk about allocation policies, pages and frames are exactly the same size
