Example: Fair scheduling. FIFO

Requests for disk sectors are served in strict arrival order.

Reminder: disk geometry is one platter, 16 cylinders, 12 sectors per cylinder, 192 sectors in total.


Sector Cylinder
100 8
3 0
150 12
23 1
120 10

The head is initially positioned at sector 0 (Cylinder 0).

All requests arrive in the table order at time 0.

Comments:

  • The head moves always looking for the next request
  • The head goes through cylinders containing requests, but not served because they are not the ones been searched
