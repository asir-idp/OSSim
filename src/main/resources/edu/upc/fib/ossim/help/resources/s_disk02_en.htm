Example: STF scheduling example. Starvation

Requests closest to the head are served first, this may cause that while requests arrive close to the head, those further away are not served (Starvation)

Reminder: disk geometry is one platter, 16 cylinders, 12 sectors per cylinder, 192 sectors in total.


Sector Cylinder Delay
170 14 0
150 12 0
120 10 0
40 3 1
20 1 2
35 2 5
2 0 6
10 0 8
50 4 15
0 0 15

The head is initially positioned at sector 50 (Cylinder 4)

All requests arrive in the table order, according to the delay indicated.

Comments:

  • The first requests are in inner cylinders, and are not served as new requests come close to the head
  • This situation could continue indefinitely
