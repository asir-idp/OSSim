Preemptive Priority scheduling example

Different duration and priority processes in the ready queue, processes have only cpu bursts, a high priority process comes later

All processes arrive in the table order, process "P5" arrive at time 2 and has priority 10.

Comments:

  • Processes are queued and sorted according to its priority, a higher value indicates a higher priority
  • At the time it arrives "P5" (Priority 10), is running "P3" (priority 9)
  • Algorithm is preemptive and therefore "P5" replaces "P3" and starts running
