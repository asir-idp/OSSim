Example: The improved elevator 1. Circular SCAN

The head movement does not depend on requests, it scans in a single direction, from the outer to the inner cylinder, the requests are served as they are found.

Reminder: disk geometry is one platter, 16 cylinders, 12 sectors per cylinder, 192 sectors in total.


Sector Cylinder
100 8
3 0
150 12
23 1
120 10

The head is initially positioned at sector 90 (Cylinder 7), moving forward innermost cylinders.

All requests arrive in the table order at time 0.

Comments:

  • Innermost request is in cylinder 12, but the head continues until the last cylinder.
  • (Improvement on SCAN) At the time it reaches the last cylinder, as it has just served the innermost requests, and those that remain are at outermost cylinders, so the head moves directly to the first cylinder and starts again
